Format Overview

Your CSV file must include a header row with specific column names to seamlessly map your sales history.

Required Columns

  • sku — The exact product SKU matching your catalog (e.g., WH-1000)
  • marketplace — The marketplace exact code (e.g., amazon, flipkart, meesho)
  • quantity — The number of units sold. Must be a positive integer
  • sale_price — The final selling price per unit. Must be numeric

Optional Columns

  • date — Sale date in YYYY-MM-DD format. Defaults to today if omitted

Do's and Don'ts

Do's
  • Ensure SKUs precisely match your catalog capitalization
  • Format dates as standard YYYY-MM-DD
  • Verify marketplace codes are active in your settings
Don'ts
  • Don't include currency symbols in sale_price
  • Don't leave the marketplace column blank
